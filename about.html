Background and Location

KeiperSchools were established since 1999 and since then have been funded by KeiperFishPLC.
Keiper Secondary school is located at Sakina in Arusha,Tanzania

Staff

Our staff totals to 90 teaching and non-teaching personel, whereby teachers oversee subjects such as
geography, history, civics,bookkeeping,commerce,english, kiswahili,physics,biology, and chemistry

Students

All primary school graduates are eligible to apply for admission at our school
where they will be equiped with Ordinary level education that is at par with the current syllabus
of The United Republic Of Tanzania (SCSEE)

Mission and Vision

The school's mission is to ensure that education brings out keen, out-spoken and disciplined students.
With our hearts, hands and minds we hope to see a generation that embraces efficient living
